Introduction

City Public Higher Secondary School, Rohri is a privately funded, independently chartered higher secondary school. Established as a Center of Excellence and served since 1996. Approved and Recognized by the Directorate Private Institutions and Board of Intermediate & Secondary Education, Sukkur. Being First & Only Private Higher Secondary School in this Region, CPHSS provides a solid foundation to prepare students to meet challenges. We are committed to achieve excellence in Quality of our Services for Parent's Satisfaction, Continual Improvement and Meeting Applicable Requirements. School management emphasizes on quality education and material should be relevant and looking to the future. The school provides an enabling environment.
